Chinchilla Lanigera

The Chinchilla Lanigera, or more informally known as the "Long tailed chinchilla", is the most common species of Chinchilla and is commonly found in captiviy of a breeder, or safely nurtured in a household environment.

Chinchilla Lanigeras are the more "domesticated" genus of chinchilla.



















Characteristics/Unique features

There isn't really all that much that seperates the Chinchilla Lanigera from the Chinchilla Chinchilla, with the main difference simply being the tail length, with Chinchilla Lanigeras tending to have longer tails than their cousins. Another difference would be that Chinchilla Lanigeras actually usually tend to be smaller than Chinchilla Chinchillas, as well as having differing body shapes, which you can see in the diagram.
